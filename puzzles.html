Teaser #3 · The Logic Puzzle

Measure exactly 4 gallons with a 3- and 5-gallon jug.

You have a 3-gallon jug and a 5-gallon jug. No measuring marks. How?

💧 Fill the 5-gallon jug. Pour into 3-gallon until full (2 gallons left in 5). Empty the 3-gallon. Pour the 2 gallons into it. Fill the 5-gallon again. Pour 1 gallon into the 3-gallon (already has 2). Now 5-gallon has exactly 4 gallons!
