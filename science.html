Experiment 1

Baking Soda Volcano

This is a simple and fun experiment to simulate a volcanic eruption using baking soda and vinegar. Here are the steps:

  1. Place a small container in the center of a tray.
  2. Fill the container halfway with baking soda.
  3. Pour vinegar over the baking soda and watch the eruption!
💡 You can add food colouring to make the eruption more colourful.

Experiment 2

Growing Crystals

In this experiment, you will grow your own crystals using salt and water. Follow these steps:

  1. Mix salt in warm water until it no longer dissolves.
  2. Pour the solution into a shallow dish.
  3. Wait for a few days and watch the crystals grow.
⏱️ Be patient – this experiment may take up to a week to see results!
