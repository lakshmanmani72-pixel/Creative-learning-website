🖌️ Today's Project: Paper Mosaic

  1. Gather colourful paper, scissors, glue, and a blank sheet.
  2. Cut or tear coloured paper into small pieces.
  3. Sketch a simple design on your blank sheet (a sun, flower, or animal).
  4. Glue the paper pieces inside your design to fill it in.
  5. Let it dry completely and display your masterpiece!
💡 Pro Tip: Use contrasting colours next to each other to make your mosaic pop. Magazine pages, wrapping paper, and even old drawings work great!
